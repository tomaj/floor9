Between schnitzel and dessert.

There’s never been a better time.

Today new models Fable 5 and Mythos 5 launch. But I want to tell you about my weekend — because sometimes even I stop understanding the speed of this world. And that’s a good thing?!

● 8 YEARS LATER
Repaying an 8-year debt
Hard to believe, but I’ve been happily married for 8 years. Quick intro — not every region in Slovakia does this the same way.
When I got married 8 years ago, I asked my friend Juraj — a good enough friend to help me out. But not that good a friend to make the wedding guest list — so he was my driver during the wedding night, shuttling guests around.
This weekend, it was his turn. He asked if I’d return the favor. And since it’s a nice break from the kids 😄, I agreed to this little recreational getaway — and repaid my debt.
● OVER THE FIRST SCHNITZEL
A problem that surfaced over food
A few hours of driving in. While eating my first schnitzel, I was thinking — when someone asks you to drive them around, during your absence people don’t know where you went, where you currently are, or how long it’ll take you to come back.
No laptop with me. But I had my phone. And on my phone, my personal Claude Code.

● MINDBLOWING
If I didn’t live in Slovakia…
…I’d be filing incorporation papers right now and launching a startup. From the car. Between schnitzel and dessert. From a phone.
● BACK TO THE POINT
Sometimes even I stop understanding
…the speed of this world and what’s now possible. I had a problem. And I don’t get it — but before I’d even finished the schnitzel, the problem was gone.
A few years ago this would have looked like: a notepad, a phone call, „I’ll call when I’m back“, „in about an hour“, uncertainty, questions. Today? Repo, description, deploy, QR, link. Everyone knows what’s going on. Live.
And if you sometimes catch yourself not keeping up with what’s now possible — congratulations. You’re doing it right. That’s not a reason for anxiety. That’s a reason to try.
● AI TIP OF THE MONTH
You don’t need a laptop. A phone and an idea will do.
Claude Code mobile + GitHub + Railway = from idea to prod URL without pulling out the laptop. Try it next time you’re in a car, on a train, or at a wedding 😉
